About Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz is a German global automobile marque and a division of Daimler AG. The brand is known for luxury vehicles, buses, coaches, and trucks. The headquarters is in Stuttgart, Baden-WÃ¼rttemberg. The name first appeared in 1926 under Daimler-Benz. In 2018, Mercedes-Benz was the largest seller of premium vehicles in the world, having sold 2.31 million passenger cars.

The history of Mercedes-Benz goes back to the creation of the first petrol-powered car by Karl Benz in 1886. Since then, Mercedes-Benz has been a pioneer in automotive engineering, consistently setting new standards for safety, comfort, and performance.
